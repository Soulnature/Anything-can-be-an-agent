## Promotion Advice

- Lead with the scale: `128 projects`, `9 domains`.
- Lead with the difference: `not just a list`, `integration-ready`, `architecture included`.
- Post updates when you add something meaningful, not only once.
- Re-share the repo when you publish `v2`, a benchmark section, or a visual system diagram.
- Highlight `results.json` often. It is one of the strongest reuse hooks in the repo.
